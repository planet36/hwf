INSTRUCTIONS

  To create a word...

./hwf-create.py riurqjeidwlp

./hwf-create.py riurqjeidwlp | ./word-value.py | sort -g


  To solve a word...

./hwf-solve.py ....a..
./hwf-solve.py .e..a..
./hwf-solve.py .e..a.. s
./hwf-solve.py .e..a.. sr
./hwf-solve.py .e..a.t sr
./hwf-solve.py .en.ant sr

"pendant" is the answer
